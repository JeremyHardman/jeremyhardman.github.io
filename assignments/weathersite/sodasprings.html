Book your flight at least 2 weeks in advance

By Sarahi Castro · gemsandpotatoes.com

Book your flight at least 2 weeks in advance I’m not kidding when I say at least two weeks. I was booking my flight to Boise, Idaho from Baltimore, Maryland and noticed flights were $143 two weeks out as opposed to $270-$500 on any other given day before. I was planning to book my flight before midnight, and not even a few seconds after the clock struck 12:00am – the flight prices changed for the day I was planning to leave. I couldn’t believe they changed right on the dot; I had to book my flight a day after my anticipated departure date.
